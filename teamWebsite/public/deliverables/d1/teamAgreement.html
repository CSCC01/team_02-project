Team Expectations Agreement

Here we outline the procedures and workflows that will be followed throughout the completion of this project.

Communication
Methods
  • Facebook Messenger
  • Zoom
  • Microsoft Teams
  • GitHub
Response Times
  • 24 Hour Response Period on Facebook
  • Arrive at Zoom/MS Teams meetings within 5 minutes of scheduled time
Meetings
Scheduling
  • Weekly Zoom meetings held on Mondays at 4:15 PM
  • Zoom meetings to be hosted by Tia, who will also take meeting minutes
  • Weekly Facebook Messenger updates will be sent on Thursdays at 3:00 PM
  • All Meetings are mandatory
Preparation
  • Each team member will be prepared with a list of updates and roadblocks before each meeting
Workflow
Division of Work
  • Will be done as a team during the Monday Zoom meeting
  • Will be documented using a Trello board
Submission of Work

The following steps will be completed before the submission of work to the master branch of the team Git repository:

  1. A Unit test suite or a list of test cases will be created per commit, where possible
  2. A commit will be ready 36 hours before the due date
  3. A commit's peer review will be completed 12 hours before the due date

Submission of work to the Git repository will be done by the group member that completed the task.

Contingency Planning — Scenarios
Team member drops out:
  • The team member is expected to inform the group as soon as possible
  • Their work will be redistributed by the team during a Zoom meeting
Team member is sick:
  • The team member is expected to inform the group as soon as possible
  • Their work will be redistributed by the team during a Zoom meeting
Team member consistently misses meetings:
  • A team meeting will be held on Zoom to discuss with the group member and understand why they are missing meetings
  • The team will work together to overcome any obstacles the individual is facing
Team member is academically dishonest
  • A team meeting will be held on Zoom to talk with the individual to understand why they were academically dishonest
  • The team will help the individual come up with original ideas and ensure they redo their work
